Slope Unblocked - Play game at school

Description

Slope Unblocked is a popular arcade game played all over the world! This game was released in 2014 by publisher Y8 and immediately gained immense popularity among gamers.

The gameplay in Slope is as simple as possible, but at the same time very addictive. You have to control the green ball, which will roll down the slope. On your way, you will encounter many difficult obstacles, among which there will be narrow tunnels with red walls, moving walls, endless pits and much more. Also, during the game, the speed of the ball falling will increase, which will give even more adrenaline to the gameplay! During the game, you will be awarded points for each completed stage. If you score too many points, you can be on the Slope leaderboard! Collect as many points as you can and take the 1st place.
